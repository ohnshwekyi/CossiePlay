What is CosPlay?

       Cosplay is a performance art in which participants called cosplayers wear costumes and fashion accessories to represent a specific character. Cosplayers often interact to create a subculture and a broader use of the term "cosplay" applies to any costumed role-playing in venues apart from the stage.

       Favorite sources include manga and anime, comic books and cartoons, video games, and live-action films and television series.Since 1990, cosplaying has made the phenomenon a significant aspect of popular culture in Japan and some other parts of Asia and in the Western world.
